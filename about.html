Our Story

Sharing our love for travel and technology.

Our team is comprised of real people that all have something in common: the love to travel. Having all been graduate students from New York University(violet pride!), we understand what it's like to plan trips on a budget. We're excited to start catching journeys for you!

We strive to bring affordability and ease-of-use to our customers when it comes to trip preparation-whether that trip is domestic or international. We achieve this by using the traveler’s customized survey results, ensuring that trips are unique to their user. Our efforts help connect travelers to the best journeys tailored to them.
